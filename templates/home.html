Welcome to the Energy Consumption Analyzer

About

The Energy Consumption Analyzer is a powerful tool that helps you track and analyze your energy usage. With our app, you can monitor your electricity, gas, and water consumption, set goals to reduce your usage, and make informed decisions to save energy and money.

Features

    • Add Energy Data
    • View Energy Data

Get Started

To start using the Energy Consumption Analyzer, simply sign up for an account and connect your energy meters to our app. Once connected, you'll be able to view your energy usage data and access all the features to help you optimize your consumption.
